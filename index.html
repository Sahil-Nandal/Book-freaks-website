Entrepreneurship

“The best way to predict the future is to create it.”

Entrepreneurship is a kind of domain, about which most of the people are curious now-a-days. And why wouldn't they be, its a century formed on the back of so many Entrepreneurs like Elon Musk, Steve Jobs, Bill Gates, Mark Zukerberg, Larry page, Peter Theil and many more. They are not only transforming our future, but building it in such a way that it can only be imagined or seen in science fiction movies.
So, most important thing is- anyone can be an entrepreneur, by having nice ideas, business and entrepreneurial skills, execution plan and may or may not be a great and powerful team.

Biographies

“Read biographies, for that is life without theory. -Benjamin Disraell”

Biographies are really important to read, because these books tell you all about a persons life, how he/she succeeded in the life, more importantly how they tackled various hurdles and many more. A person who you think as your role model and wanted to be like him/her, either professionally of generally as a person, then biography of that great personalty can teach you all the things and gives you a chance to dive deep into their lives to explore and learn tons of life lessons.

Self-Help

“Books that talks about life through beautiful fables”

A successful self-help book author establishes a relationship with you by writing a book that's relatively easy to use, makes you feel that there's hope for you, shows you that the author understands your problems, and gives you at least some advice for life right away. These books encourages you to focus on your goals, figure out strategies on achieving those goals, overcoming obstacles that delay your achievements, and staying inspired all the way long.
